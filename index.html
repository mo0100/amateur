Best Pizza Ever

Must have

  • Pizza stone or Pizza steel ,I prefer pizza steel it get hotter than pizza stone.

    Ingredients for pizza dough

    • 500 Grams of 00 flour or bread flour , 00 flour will make your pizza very soft like bitting into a cloud
    • 1 tsp of active dry yeast ,if you have fresh yeast even better but I don't know how much to use ,never trird it.
    • 350 ML of water .not warm and not cold ,just water.
    • 2 tsp salt
      • that's it ,that's all you need for the best dough ever.

    Instructions

    1. Mix flour,yeat together first for a minute ,then add half the water ,pay attention just half don't put all the water at once .you can do that in a bowl or in a mixer .mixer is way easier .obvisely .
    2. after about 3 to 4 minutes add a little bit of water ,keep adding water bit by bit while mixing for about 20 minutes until all dough get together in the mixer as one pieace.
    3. Knead the dough until smooth and elastic.
    4. now,leave the dough coverd for an hour .
    5. time flies,after one hour cut dough to three equal part .it will make about 280 Grams per ball ,make dough round as a ball by folding the dough.
    6. put the pizza balls in airtigh containers and place in fride ,yes fridge ,keep in fridge for 24 hours if you want to keep it there for a week .go nuts.
    7. after 24 hours ,I keep mine for 48 hours btw, take the dough out of fridge and let get to room tempature about one to two hours .I don't know how cold or hot your room is.
    8. while waiting on the dough place your pizza stone in the middle rack,turn on your oven on the highest it can get .500F or 550 .
    9. touch your pizza dough to make sure it's room tempeture then add some flour to the work are ,and spred your dough by pushing air from the center to the edges to create the crust
    10. add your favorate pizza sauce , and place your pizza in the oven,yes I know I haven't added any topping yet .home ovens don't get super hot so you have to do two s.
    11. keep an eye on the pizza because it won't take long at this stage ,once the crust starts to get some color about 3 or 4 minutes take the pizza out .

    13. let it cool for a minute ,that cheese will burn your mouth. Enjoy ,that should be the best pizza you ever had .
